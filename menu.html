AI Mastery for Teachers

A challenge series for AI confidence and workflow mastery

All activities can be undertaken using free accounts

Welcome! This programme is designed to take you from AI-curious to AI-confident through two stages. Work through them in order to build a solid foundation of safe practice before exploring the creative and practical power of AI in your classroom.

Stage 1

Safe & Responsible Use

A 30 scenario-based question assessment covering data protection, safeguarding, copyright, academic integrity and responsible classroom use. The quiz reflects your learning from Department for Education guidance. You need 90% to pass and receive your certificate.

🔗 Access the DfE guidance on using AI in education here before taking the assessment
